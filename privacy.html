1. Privacy at SAPYENS

At SAPYENS, we believe your personal information belongs to you.

Ziko AI is designed to help you achieve your nutrition and fitness goals through personalized features powered by modern technology and artificial intelligence. To provide these features, we need to collect and process certain information that you choose to share with us.

We are committed to being transparent about how we collect, use, store, and protect your information. We collect only the information that is reasonably necessary to provide and improve the Service, and we do not sell your personal information.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, when it may be shared, and the choices and rights you have regarding your information.

Our goal is to present this information in clear and understandable language so you can make informed decisions about your privacy when using Ziko AI.

2. Scope of This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to:

  • The Ziko AI mobile application;
  • The SAPYENS website;
  • Communications between you and SAPYENS regarding Ziko AI; and
  • All related products, features, and services offered through Ziko AI (collectively, the "Service").

This Privacy Policy does not apply to third-party websites, applications, or services that are governed by their own privacy policies.

3. Who We Are

Ziko AI is developed and operated under the SAPYENS brand.

For legal purposes, the Service is operated by Fabio Habchi, conducting business under the commercial name SAPYENS.

Throughout this Privacy Policy, the terms "SAPYENS", "we", "us", and "our" refer to the operator of the Service.

By creating an account or using Ziko AI, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.

If you do not agree with this Privacy Policy, you should not access or use the Service.

4. Definitions

For the purposes of this Privacy Policy:

Account means your registered Ziko AI account.

Personal Information means any information that identifies, relates to, describes, or can reasonably be associated with an identifiable individual.

Health Information means wellness-related information that you choose to provide through the Service, including nutrition, body measurements, workout information, fitness goals, and progress tracking data.

User Content means any information or content that you voluntarily submit through the Service, including text, images, voice recordings, meal photographs, progress photographs, and other uploaded content.

AI Features means the artificial intelligence-powered functionality available within Ziko AI, including meal analysis, recipe generation, workout generation, and other personalized features.

Third-Party Services means trusted external providers that help us operate the Service, including cloud infrastructure providers, authentication providers, payment processors, artificial intelligence providers, email delivery providers, and other technology partners.

Service means the Ziko AI mobile application, the SAPYENS website, and all related products and services provided under the SAPYENS brand.

You or User means any individual who accesses or uses the Service.

5. Information We Collect

The information we collect depends on how you use Ziko AI and the features you choose to use. Some information is provided directly by you, some is generated as you use the Service, and some is collected automatically to help us operate the Service securely and reliably.

5.1. Information You Provide

Account Information

When you create an account, we may collect:

  • Username
  • Email address
  • Encrypted password (when using email sign-in)
  • Optional profile picture
  • Authentication method (Email, Google Sign-In, or Sign in with Apple)

This information is used to create, manage, and secure your account.

Profile and Personalization Information

To personalize your experience, you may choose to provide:

  • Age
  • Gender
  • Height
  • Current weight
  • Goal weight
  • Activity level
  • Fitness goal (such as weight loss, muscle gain, or maintenance)
  • Dietary preferences
  • Food restrictions
  • Workout experience
  • Available equipment
  • Preferred workout location (home or gym)
  • Available workout days
  • Preferred workout duration
  • Injury information that you voluntarily choose to share

Providing this information is optional; however, some personalized features may be unavailable or less accurate if certain information is not provided.

Nutrition and Fitness Information

As you use Ziko AI, you may create and store information including:

  • Meals you log
  • Meal photographs
  • Estimated nutritional information
  • Calories
  • Macronutrients
  • Micronutrients
  • Weight history
  • Optional progress photographs
  • Workout history
  • Workout plans created or saved through the Service

This information allows you to monitor your progress and enables personalized recommendations throughout the Service.

User Content

Depending on the features you use, you may voluntarily submit content such as:

  • Meal photographs
  • Progress photographs
  • Text describing meals
  • Voice recordings used for meal logging
  • Messages submitted when using AI-powered features

We process this content solely to provide the features you request.

5.2. Information Collected Automatically

To operate Ziko AI securely and reliably, we may automatically collect limited technical information, including:

  • Device type
  • Operating system version
  • Application version
  • Device language
  • Authentication logs
  • Security-related information
  • General diagnostic information

This information helps us maintain security, troubleshoot technical issues, and improve compatibility across supported devices.

5.3. Permissions You Choose to Grant

Certain features require permission to access capabilities of your device.

Camera

Used to capture meal photographs and optional progress photographs.

Photo Library

Used to upload existing meal photographs or progress photographs from your device.

Microphone

Used to record voice input for meal logging.

Notifications

Used to send reminders and important service-related notifications.

You may grant, deny, or revoke these permissions at any time through your device settings. Please note that disabling certain permissions may prevent some features from functioning as intended.

5.4. Purchase Information

If you purchase a subscription, payments are processed exclusively by the Apple App Store or Google Play Store.

We do not receive or store your payment card information.

We receive only the limited information necessary to verify your subscription status and provide access to premium features.

5.5. Information We Do Not Collect

We believe transparency also means explaining what we do not collect.

Ziko AI does not:

  • Access or track your precise location.
  • Access your contacts.
  • Access your text messages.
  • Access your call history.
  • Receive or store your payment card information.
  • Access files or photographs stored on your device unless you explicitly choose to share them with the Service.

6. How We Use Your Information

We use your information only for purposes that are necessary to provide, maintain, improve, and protect Ziko AI. The way your information is used depends on the features you choose to use.

We use your information to:

  • Authenticate your identity when you sign in.
  • Synchronize your information across your devices.
  • Provide access to free and premium features.
  • Save your preferences and personalized settings.
  • Deliver the functionality you request.

6.2. To Personalize Your Experience

One of the core purposes of Ziko AI is to provide a personalized nutrition and fitness experience.

We use the information you provide to:

  • Calculate your nutritional targets.
  • Generate personalized workout plans.
  • Tailor recommendations for your goals and preferences.
  • Track your progress over time.
  • Generate personalized insights.
  • Improve the relevance of AI-powered features.

The quality of these personalized features depends on the accuracy and completeness of the information you choose to provide.

6.3. To Process AI-Powered Features

When you choose to use AI-powered features, we process the information necessary to provide the functionality you request.

Depending on the feature being used, this may include:

  • Meal photographs.
  • Voice input.
  • Text input.
  • Relevant profile information.
  • Nutrition and fitness information needed to generate personalized results.

We process only the information that is reasonably necessary to deliver the requested AI-powered feature.

Additional information about AI-powered functionality is provided in our AI Disclaimer.

6.4. To Improve Ziko AI

We continuously improve Ziko AI to provide a better experience for our users.

Your information may be used to:

  • Improve application performance.
  • Maintain compatibility with supported devices.
  • Fix software bugs.
  • Enhance existing features.
  • Develop new features.
  • Improve the quality and reliability of our nutrition, fitness, and AI-powered functionality.

Where possible, improvements are based on aggregated or non-identifying information rather than information that directly identifies individual users.

6.5. To Communicate With You

We may use your information to communicate with you regarding your account or your use of Ziko AI.

These communications may include:

  • Account verification.
  • Password reset emails.
  • Security notifications.
  • Important updates about the Service.
  • Changes to this Privacy Policy or other legal documents.
  • Responses to support requests.

These communications are considered necessary for providing the Service.

6.6. Notifications

With your permission, Ziko AI may send notifications to help you stay engaged with your nutrition and fitness goals.

7. AI Features

Artificial intelligence is a core part of Ziko AI and enables features such as meal analysis, personalized workout generation, recipe suggestions, and other intelligent assistance designed to enhance your experience.

We believe it is important to be transparent about how AI is used within the Service.

7.1. When AI Is Used

AI-powered processing occurs only when you choose to use features that require it.

Depending on the feature you use, this may include:

  • Analyzing meal photographs.
  • Processing meal descriptions entered as text.
  • Processing voice input used for meal logging.
  • Generating personalized workout plans.
  • Generating recipe suggestions.

AI processing is performed only to provide the functionality you have requested.

7.2. Information Processed by AI

To provide AI-powered features, we may process information including:

  • Meal photographs.
  • Voice recordings submitted for meal logging.
  • Text that you enter.
  • Relevant profile information.
  • Nutrition and fitness information necessary to generate personalized results.

Only the information reasonably necessary to fulfill your request is processed.

7.3. How AI Results Are Used

The results generated by AI are used exclusively to provide the requested feature within Ziko AI.

Examples include:

  • Identifying foods.
  • Estimating nutritional information.
  • Generating workout plans.
  • Suggesting recipes.
  • Personalizing recommendations.

AI-generated results become part of your account only when they are saved within the Service.

7.4. Human Review

AI-generated requests and responses are processed automatically.

SAPYENS does not routinely review your meal photographs, voice recordings, or AI interactions.

Access to user information is limited to authorized personnel only when reasonably necessary to maintain, secure, troubleshoot, or improve the Service, or when required by applicable law.

7.5. AI Limitations

While we strive to provide accurate and helpful AI-powered features, artificial intelligence may occasionally generate inaccurate, incomplete, or unexpected results.

For this reason, AI-generated content should be considered informational and should not be relied upon as professional medical, nutritional, or fitness advice.

Additional information is available in our AI Disclaimer and Medical Disclaimer.

7.6. Our Commitment Regarding AI

We are committed to using artificial intelligence responsibly.

Accordingly:

  • We do not use your personal information to train proprietary AI models developed by SAPYENS.
  • We process only the information reasonably necessary to provide the AI feature you request.
  • We do not use AI to make fully automated decisions that produce legal or similarly significant effects on you.
  • We continuously evaluate our AI-powered features to improve their reliability, safety, and overall user experience.

9. Protecting and Managing Your Information

Protecting your information is a fundamental part of how we build and operate Ziko AI.

We use industry-standard technical, administrative, and organizational safeguards designed to help protect your personal information from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, alteration, or disclosure.

As technology evolves, we continuously review and improve our security practices to help keep your information protected.

9.1. How Your Information Is Stored

Information associated with your account is securely stored using cloud infrastructure operated on behalf of SAPYENS.

Depending on how you use Ziko AI, this may include:

  • Account information;
  • Nutrition and wellness information;
  • Workout information;
  • Meal photographs;
  • Progress photographs;
  • Subscription status;
  • AI-generated content that you choose to save; and
  • Other information you voluntarily store within the Service.

We store only the information reasonably necessary to operate, maintain, and improve the Service.

9.2. Security Measures

To help protect your information, we implement security measures including, where appropriate:

  • Encrypted connections (HTTPS);
  • Secure authentication systems;
  • Encrypted password storage;
  • Role-based access controls;
  • Secure cloud infrastructure; and
  • Ongoing monitoring of the security and reliability of the Service.

Access to personal information is restricted to authorized systems and, where necessary, authorized personnel who require access to operate, maintain, or support the Service.

9.3. How Long We Keep Your Information

We retain your information only for as long as it is reasonably necessary to:

  • Provide the Service;
  • Maintain your account;
  • Deliver the features you choose to use;
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations;
  • Resolve disputes;
  • Protect the security and integrity of the Service; or
  • Enforce our legal agreements.

The length of time information is retained depends on the type of information and the purpose for which it was collected.

9.4. Account Deletion

You may request deletion of your account at any time through the settings available within Ziko AI.

When an account deletion request is submitted:

  • Your account enters a 14-day pending deletion period.
  • During this period, your account remains recoverable if you sign back into Ziko AI.
  • Signing back into your account during the pending deletion period automatically cancels the deletion request and restores your account.

If no login occurs during the 14-day period, your account and associated personal information are permanently deleted from our systems, except where limited retention is required by applicable law.

9.5. Deleting Your Nutrition and Workout Data

You may choose to delete certain categories of information without deleting your account.

Where supported by the Service, this includes nutrition history, workout history, and associated records.

Deleting this information may affect personalized recommendations and other features that rely on your historical data.

Where applicable, meal photographs and progress photographs associated with deleted nutrition records will also be permanently removed.

9.6. Voice Recordings

Voice recordings submitted for meal logging are processed only for the purpose of providing the requested feature.

Voice recordings are not retained after processing.

9.7. Our Security Commitment

Protecting your privacy is an ongoing responsibility.

While no online service can guarantee absolute security, SAPYENS is committed to applying recognized security practices and continuously improving the safeguards used to protect your information.

We regularly evaluate and enhance our security measures to help maintain the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of the information entrusted to us.
