1. Account & Data Management

At Ziko AI, we believe you should remain in control of your information. You can delete your account or remove your nutrition and workout data directly from within the app at any time.

2. Delete Your Account

You can permanently delete your Ziko AI account directly from the app.

2.1. How to delete your account

  1. Open Ziko AI.
  2. Sign in to your account.
  3. Go to Settings.
  4. Tap Delete Account.
  5. Confirm your deletion request.

2.2. What happens after requesting account deletion?

  • Your account enters a 14-day pending deletion period.
  • During these 14 days, you can cancel the deletion simply by signing back into your account.
  • If you do not sign in during the 14-day period, your account will be permanently deleted.

2.3. What is permanently deleted?

Once the deletion becomes permanent, the following information is removed from our systems:

  • Your account information
  • Profile information
  • Nutrition history
  • Workout history
  • Meal logs
  • Meal photographs
  • Progress photographs
  • Weight history
  • AI-generated content saved to your account
  • Any other personal information associated with your account

After permanent deletion, this information cannot be recovered.

3. Delete Nutrition and Workout Data

If you would like to keep your account but remove your personal tracking history, you can delete your nutrition and workout data without deleting your account.

3.1. How to delete your data

  1. Open Ziko AI.
  2. Sign in to your account.
  3. Go to Settings.
  4. Select Delete Nutrition & Workout Data.
  5. Confirm your request.

3.2. What is deleted?

Deleting your nutrition and workout data permanently removes:

  • Nutrition history
  • Meal logs
  • Meal photographs
  • Weight history
  • Progress photographs
  • Workout history
  • Workout plans
  • Related nutrition and workout records

Your account, email address, subscription status (if applicable), and basic profile information remain unchanged.

Please note that deleting this information may affect personalized recommendations and other features that rely on your historical data.
