Questions

Is Ziko AI free?

Yes. Ziko AI offers a free plan that includes 20 credits to explore its core features. For users who want more, Premium includes 200 credits per month, while Platinum offers unlimited access. Every AI-powered action uses a small number of credits, so you only pay for the level of usage that fits your needs.

Does Ziko AI create personalized plans?

Yes. Every nutrition and workout recommendation is tailored to your goals, preferences, activity level, and progress. As you use the app, Ziko AI continuously adapts its guidance to keep your plan relevant and effective.

Can I use Ziko AI only for nutrition?

Absolutely. Whether you're looking for nutrition guidance, personalized workouts, or both, Ziko AI adapts to the way you want to use it. You decide which features fit your journey.

Is my data private?

Yes. Your privacy is a priority. Personal data is securely stored and used only to provide and improve your experience within Ziko AI. We never sell your personal information, and you remain in control of your data at all times.
